Game Over!

White Wins!

After 178 moves, the black team has voted to end the game by resignation. Congratulations to the white team on their victory! It was an intense game with a fairly technical opening and a grueling middle game riddled with large trades.

I want to show my appreciation to all of the participants on both sides, from strong Dan players giving invaluable advice to newer players looking who have immersed themselves into this game.


What Next?

For the time being, the site will be up and running, so that everyone may view the move sequence on the board, the chat will still be open for use, and the SGF can still be downloaded for viewing on a better (yes, I know) game replay app. Voting and commenting have been disabled.

As promised, the game will be reviewed by Jiang MingJiu 7 Dan Professional. We should have this done within a couple of weeks; Mr. Jiang's schedule permitting. I will also release some comprehensive statistics about this game in a pretty infographic.

We will once again go into a short period of furious development on the server in anticipation of the next game. Expect the experience in the next game to be even better!

If you have would like to be notified about when the next game starts, of Jiang MingJiu's review, or have any feedback about ConGo, please fill out this survey: Survey Monkey


Some More Words
